Travel Green, Leave Only Footprints!

Small actions make a big difference ! Follow these eco-friendly travel tips to preserve the Earth.

Travel smarter, not faster +

Opt for trains, buses, or sailing boats instead of planes or cruise ships. You'll cut your emissions and enjoy scenic routes and local experiences along the way.

Discover local wonders +

Explore nearby parks, villages, or natural reserves. You'll save money, reduce travel emissions, and might be surprised by the hidden gems around you.

Explore under your own power +

Trade engines for energy! Go hiking, biking, or kayaking, to connect deeply with the places you visit.

Leave nothing but footprints +

Pack a reusable bottle, avoid single-use plastic and clean up after yourself, that's so easy and it can help the planet so much !

Support local, live like a local +

Choose local guides, stay in eco-lodges, eat at family-run restaurants and buy locally made products. Your money helps preserve local culture and creates sustainable jobs.

Respect wildlife and wild places +

Admire animals from a distance, stay on marked paths and avoid feeding or disturbing the species : they deserve their peace as much as we do.
